Aliens not only have large eyes, but they have large dark eyes.

  1. Use the Quick Mask mode to select your eyes (reread Step 3 if you don’t remember how to use the Quick Mask mode)...

  2. Press the Q key to exit Quick Mask mode and then invert the selection - just your eyes should now be selected

  3. Click Image then point at Adjustments and click Brightness/Contrast...

  4. Move the Contrast slider all the way to the right, then move the Brightness slider to the left to darken the eyes until you get something you like...

    For my character, I used a Brightness of -50 and a Contrast of 100...

    Remember that your numbers may be different from mine

  5. Click OK

  6. Press Ctrl+D to deselect your image

Your eyes probably have have an edge on them now, sooooooo:

  1. Use the Blur Tool to go around the edge of both of your eyes and fix the hard edge

  2. Save your work in your Alien folder
